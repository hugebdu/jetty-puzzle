Instructions:                                

Choose what puzzle you want to solve and play the old sliding puzzle game against an opponent

Once puzzle the was chosen send your opponent the link that you were redirected to (copy from browser)

Once your opponent entered the link the game has begun – SOLVE THE PUZZLE

 

During the game you and your opponent will simultaneously encounter challenges of three types:

1.       Prisoners

a.       If you both choose the dilemma it will harm both your boards

b.      If only one of you choose the dilemma it will do a big damage to the other player

c.       If no one chooses this dilemma it will harm you both a little bit

2.       Chicken

a.       If you both choose the dilemma it will harm both your boards a little bit

b.      If only one of you choose the dilemma it will do a big damage to him

c.       If no one chooses the dilemma it will do a big damage to both boards

3.       Boom

a.       Firs one to choose this challenge will harm the board of the opponent

You will have to react within 5 seconds

First one to solve the puzzle with all of those dilemmas wins
